Corvette C4

1984

  -1996

This generation marked a rethinking of the "American Sports car" following the stagnant design of the C3. Easily one of the most anticipated launches, the new design of the C4 led by Chief Engineer David McLellan Lead Designer and Jerry Palmer was modern take on the classic car. The result was not only a re-design, but a brand-new car with the "look and feel" of the generations beforehand.
