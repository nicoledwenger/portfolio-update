Corvette C7

2014

  -2019

Following a financial crisis, the seventh generation was faced with financial restraints. Tom Peters, designer, was faced with simply giving the sixth generation an evolutionary look rather than gaining free reign. The C7 was put on hold due to the financial crisis, but this allowed for ample time to look at the design and create a distinctive Corvette look. Overall, the seventh generation found a balance between innovation and maintaining the legacy of the Corvette.
