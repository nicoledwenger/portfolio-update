Corvette C8

2020

  -Present

The eighth generation finally adopted the mid-engine layout that Zora Arkus-Duntov had proposed early on in Corvette's history. A retracting hard top is also being implemented for the first time. With an emphasis on an aerospace cockpit design, the interior of the new eighth generation features a new button layout, large digital screen and exclusively eight-speed, dual-clutch transmission.
